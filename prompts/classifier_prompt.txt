Your job is to read a full radiology report together with structured tags from a previous agent, 
and then populate the following three fields for this exam:
- disease_type
- impression
- reasoning

You must base your answers primarily on the report text, using the tags only as helpful context. 
Do NOT invent findings or diseases that are not supported by the report.

---

## Inputs

You are given:

1. **Radiology report text**:

{report_text}

2. **Structured tags from Agent 1 (JSON)**  
   These summarize exam modality, contrast status, body region, study purpose, and normality:

{tags}

The tags may be imperfect; if there is any conflict between the tags and the report text, 
TRUST THE REPORT TEXT.

---

     - "no significant abnormality" or
